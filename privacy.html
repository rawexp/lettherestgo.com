Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Let The Rest Go does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your information stays on your device.

What Data We Collect

None. Let The Rest Go does not collect any personal information, usage data, or analytics.

How Your Data is Stored

All data you create in Let The Rest Go (your focus items and completed archive) is stored locally on your device using iOS's built-in storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Is included in your iCloud backup if you have device backups enabled

Third-Party Services

Let The Rest Go does not use any third-party services, analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

Internet Connection

Let The Rest Go does not require an internet connection and does not transmit any data over the network.

Widgets

Let The Rest Go includes home screen widgets that display your current focus item. Widget data is shared between the app and widget using a secure App Group container on your device. This data remains entirely local.

Children's Privacy

Let The Rest Go does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this page. Since Let The Rest Go doesn't collect data, we don't anticipate any changes.
